Exoplanets

What are they?

Exoplanets are all planets that orbit another star, which is not the sun. There are free floating planets that once orbited a star, but have been ejected from the system they are from.

(Source: http://exoplanets.org/methodology.html)

Known Exoplanets

Kepler-186f -first rocky planet to be found in a habitable zone, which means that the temperature ist right for liquid water.

Kepler-16b -first planet discovered that orbits two stars.

Kepler-22b -planet in habitable zone and possible water-world planet.
